  • Jared Jestr is a South African born artist specialising in pressure-based painting mediums. Having practiced graffiti for 17 years, Jestr has developed an instantly recognisable painting style that transcends traditional graffiti, seeking to blur definition and dimensions. His work is featured in urban art museums in Sweden and Denmark, and commission clients include Ogilvy, The city of Johannesburg, and Virgin Airlines. He hope to inspire others and build digital community for urban arts.
